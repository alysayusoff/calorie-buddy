About CalorieBuddy

CalorieBuddy is a website that functions as a digital calorie counter to help you manage your diet! You are given the choice to add nutritional facts for food ingredients and to store them in the database. CalorieBuddy will provide you with the sum of the calories for a recipe or a meal based on the food ingredients selected by you to be included in a recipe or meal.
